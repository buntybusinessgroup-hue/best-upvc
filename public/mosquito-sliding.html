Mosquito Sliding Mesh

Enjoy natural breeze and sunlight without the worry of mosquitoes and insects.

Seamless Protection

In regions like Hyderabad, mosquito protection is essential. Our integrated sliding mesh systems are specifically designed to fit seamlessly with our uPVC windows and doors without compromising aesthetics.

Maximum Airflow

The fine mesh is woven tightly enough to keep the smallest insects out while still allowing up to 80% natural airflow and ventilation into your home.

Smooth Operation

Mounted on an independent dedicated track within the uPVC frame, the mesh panel slides effortlessly and independently of the glass panels.

Easy Maintenance

The high-grade fiberglass or stainless steel mesh is dust-resistant and can be easily washed or wiped down without stretching or tearing.

Technical Specifications

  • Mesh Material: High-tensile PVC-coated fiberglass or premium SS (Stainless Steel) options available.
  • Frame Integration: Built directly into a 2.5-track or 3-track uPVC frame system for a unified look.
  • Visibility: Highly transparent weave that does not block outside views.
  • Durability: Rot-proof, rust-proof, and highly resistant to accidental tearing from pets or children.
  • Application: Compatible with both sliding windows and sliding balcony doors.
