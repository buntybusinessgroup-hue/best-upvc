Why Choose Our UPVC Solutions?

Modern homes require robust solutions. Our profiles are meticulously engineered to provide superior structural integrity while maximizing light and minimizing energy loss.

100% Soundproofing

Double glazed Prominance Windows are highly effective in reducing noise pollution. Perfect for homes near busy roads or airports, ensuring a peaceful indoor environment.

Thermal Insulation

Reduce energy loss by over 30%. Our multi-chambered UPVC profiles keep your home cooler in summer and warmer in winter, drastically cutting your AC electricity bills.

Weather & UV Resistant

Engineered with the right amount of TiO2, our windows offer 100% UV resistance. They retain their pristine whiteness and will not warp, rot, or fade even in extreme Indian summers and monsoons.

Zero Maintenance

Unlike wooden frames, our UPVC windows are termite-proof and never require painting or polishing. A simple wipe down is all it takes to keep them looking brand new for decades.
