Our Window Variants

We manufacture and install a wide variety of uPVC window styles tailored to your architectural needs and functional requirements.

Sliding Windows

Perfect for spaces with limited room. Our sliding systems glide effortlessly on high-grade nylon rollers, offering expansive views and excellent ventilation.

Casement Windows

Traditional outwards-opening windows offering maximum ventilation and a tighter seal against wind and rain when closed. Ideal for bedrooms and living rooms.

Tilt & Turn Windows

European-style functionality allowing the window to tilt inwards for draft-free ventilation or open fully like a casement for cleaning and maximum airflow.

Technical Specifications

  • Material: Unplasticized Polyvinyl Chloride (uPVC) blended with TiO2 for UV resistance.
  • Profile Thickness: Multi-chambered designs (3 to 5 chambers) for maximum insulation.
  • Glazing Options: Single (5mm/6mm), Double Glazed (DGU), and Toughened Glass.
  • Hardware: European-standard multi-point locking systems for enhanced security.
  • Acoustics: Reduces outside noise by up to 30-40 decibels (with DGU).
  • Lifespan: Tested for 25,000+ hours in extreme Indian climate without fading or warping.
